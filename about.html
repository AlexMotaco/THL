    ABOUT US

    Town Hall Lanes is the largest duckpin center in Rhode Island, with 32 lanes. It was one of the first bowling centers with fully automated pin-setters when it opened and has since become a popular destination for bowlers of all ages and skill levels. From the moment you step through our doors, you'll feel the energy and excitement of a place that's all about fun. Founded in 1959 by Anthony and Anna Zanni, our alley has always been a place where people can come to relax, have a good time, and make lasting memories. In addition to our lanes and activities, we also offer a fully stocked bar & restaurant, as well as a large youth bowling program and sponsors for adults and senior leagues. So if you're looking for a great place to bowl, socialize, and have fun, come visit us at THL Social. We can't wait to bowl wih you!
