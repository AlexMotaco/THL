    Cosmic Bowling

    WHATS COSMIC BOWLING?

    Are you looking for a fun and unique way to spend your Saturday night? Come join us for cosmic bowling at Town Hall Lanes! This exciting event combines the fun of bowling with colorful lights, lasers, and great music to create an unforgettable experience. Not only will you have a blast bowling with your friends and family, but you'll also have the chance to win prizes and enjoy tasty food and drinks from our on-site restaurant & bar. So why wait? Grab your bowling shoes and come join us for a night of cosmic fun! You won't want to miss it.

    LIGHT SHOWS

    During a cosmic bowling event, the lights in the bowling alley are typically turned down or turned off completely, and the special lighting effects are used to illuminate the lanes and create a unique atmosphere.

    GREAT MUSIC

    The music played during a cosmic bowling event is often upbeat and energetic, and may include a mix of popular songs from various genres. The music is changed to match the mood and energy of the event.
